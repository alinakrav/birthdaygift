Drop your images in THIS folder using these exact filenames.
Until you add them, the page shows a fruit placeholder, so it never
looks broken while you're getting the photos ready.

The cards have NO text on the front — just the photo. The description
lives on the BACK of each card (flip with the "↻ note" pill).

Required files (6 options)
--------------------------
option1.jpg   -> Deep navy leather tote  = YOUR PICK
                 (this same photo also pops out of the gift box on slide 3)
option2.jpg   -> Blue leather tote
option3.jpg   -> Cream white leather tote
option4.jpg   -> Brown leather tote
option5.jpg   -> Beige Longchamp tote
option6.jpg   -> Navy Longchamp tote

Placeholders (shown until you add real photos)
----------------------------------------------
option1 = 🍎 apple   option2 = 🍊 orange   option3 = 🍌 banana
option4 = 🍐 pear     option5 = 🍇 grapes   option6 = 🍓 strawberry

Tips
----
- .jpg or .png both work, but keep the names above (or update the
  src="images/..." paths in index.html to match your filenames).
- Square-ish images look best (they're cropped to fill the card).
- To change the note on a card's back, open index.html and search
  "note-back". To change what gets emailed, edit each card's data-name.
